A balcony is a platform that extends from the wall of a building, typically above the ground floor, and is enclosed by a railing or balustrade. Balconies are often used as an outdoor space for relaxation, enjoying views, or growing plants. They can be found in various architectural styles and are common in residential buildings, hotels, and other structures. Balconies can vary in size and design, ranging from small Juliet balconies to larger ones that can accommodate furniture and gatherings. They provide a connection to the outdoors and can enhance the aesthetic appeal of a building. Balconies are a popular feature in modern architecture and can be designed to suit a variety of styles.
